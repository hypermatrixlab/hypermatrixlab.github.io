## Prospective Students

We welcome motivated students interested in Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, and Trustworthy AI. If you're interested in joining our lab:

- **PhD/MSc applicants**: Please apply through the [Dalhousie CS Graduate Program](https://www.dal.ca/faculty/computerscience/graduate-programs.html) and mention Prof. Hassan in your application
- **Undergraduate students**: Please send your CV and transcript to Prof. Hassan
- **Visiting researchers**: Please email Prof. Hassan with your research interests and proposed visit dates

## Visiting Us

We are located on the **4th floor** of the Faculty of Computer Science building at Dalhousie University's Studley Campus.

**Parking**: Visitor parking is available at the nearby [Studley Parking](https://www.dal.ca/campus-maps/parking/parking-maps.html)

**Public Transit**: The building is accessible via [Halifax Transit](https://www.halifax.ca/transportation/halifax-transit) routes serving the Studley Campus
